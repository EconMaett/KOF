The subdirectories contain both the data and R code to reproduce the results presented in Sections 4.2 and 5 of Abberger et al. (2022).


Abberger, K., M. Graff, O. Müller and J.-E. Sturm (2022), Composite global indicators from survey data: the Global Economic Barometers, Review of World Economics, https://doi.org/10.1007/s10290-021-00449-8.
